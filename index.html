Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about X Video Embedder

What is X Video Embedder?

X Video Embedder is a browser extension that allows you to seamlessly embed videos from other X (Twitter) users onto your own X profile. It simplifies the process of sharing and showcasing video content, making it easier to curate and display videos that resonate with your audience without needing to repost or download content.

Which browsers are supported?

X Video Embedder is compatible with all major browsers including:

  • Google Chrome (version 88+)
  • Mozilla Firefox (version 85+)
  • Microsoft Edge (version 88+)
  • Opera (version 74+)
  • Brave Browser
  • Any other Chromium-based browser
Is my X account information secure?

Absolutely! We take security very seriously. X Video Embedder never stores your X credentials on our servers. All authentication is handled through official X OAuth protocols, and your login information remains solely between you and X. The extension only requests the minimum permissions necessary to embed videos on your behalf. We are fully compliant with data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA.

Can I embed videos from private accounts?

No, X Video Embedder only works with publicly available video content. Videos from private or protected accounts cannot be embedded due to X's privacy policies and API restrictions. This ensures that content creators' privacy settings are always respected.

How many videos can I embed?

There are no hard limits imposed by the extension itself. However, X may have rate limits on API calls to prevent spam. We recommend embedding videos thoughtfully and not exceeding 50-100 videos per day. For power users with higher needs, please contact us for enterprise solutions.

What happens if the original video is deleted?

If the original video is deleted by its owner or removed for any reason, the embedded video on your profile will also be removed automatically. This ensures compliance with content ownership and prevents broken links. You'll receive a notification in the extension dashboard when this occurs.

Does this violate any X terms of service?

No, X Video Embedder operates within X's terms of service and API guidelines. The extension uses official X APIs and embed functionalities that are publicly available. We do not download, re-upload, or modify video content‚Äîwe simply create embed links that reference the original content, similar to how you would manually share or quote a post.

Can I schedule video embeds?

Yes! The premium version of X Video Embedder includes a scheduling feature that allows you to queue up videos and have them automatically embedded at specific times. This is perfect for content creators who want to maintain a consistent posting schedule.

Is there a mobile app version?

Currently, X Video Embedder is available as a browser extension for desktop browsers. We're actively developing mobile app versions for iOS and Android, which will be released in the coming months. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when mobile apps become available.

How do I uninstall the extension?

To uninstall X Video Embedder:

  • Chrome/Edge: Go to Extensions ‚Üí Manage Extensions ‚Üí Find X Video Embedder ‚Üí Click Remove
  • Firefox: Go to Add-ons ‚Üí Extensions ‚Üí Find X Video Embedder ‚Üí Click Remove

All embedded videos will remain on your profile even after uninstalling, but you won't be able to add new embeds or manage existing ones through the extension.

Troubleshooting

Solutions to common issues and problems

Extension Not Appearing

If the X Video Embedder icon doesn't appear in your browser toolbar:

  1. Ensure the extension is properly installed and enabled in your browser's extension settings
  2. Try refreshing the X webpage or restarting your browser
  3. Check if you're using a supported browser version
  4. Disable conflicting extensions that might interfere with X Video Embedder
  5. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then reload

Cannot Embed Video

If you're unable to embed a specific video:

  1. Verify the video is from a public account and publicly visible
  2. Check if the video violates X's content policies
  3. Ensure you're logged into your X account in the browser
  4. Try re-authorizing the extension in the settings
  5. Check if you've reached X's daily API rate limits
  6. Wait a few minutes and try again

Embedded Video Not Displaying

If an embedded video isn't showing on your profile:

  1. Refresh your X profile page (Ctrl+F5 for hard refresh)
  2. Check if the original video is still available
  3. Verify your internet connection is stable
  4. Try viewing your profile in an incognito/private window
  5. Clear your browser cache and reload the page
  6. Check X's status page for any platform-wide issues

Slow Performance Issues

If the extension is running slowly or causing lag:

  1. Close unnecessary browser tabs to free up memory
  2. Disable other extensions temporarily to identify conflicts
  3. Update your browser to the latest version
  4. Clear browser cache and temporary files
  5. Restart your browser completely
  6. Check your computer's available RAM and CPU usage

Authentication Problems

If you're having trouble connecting your X account:

  1. Log out of X completely and log back in
  2. Revoke X Video Embedder's access in X Settings ‚Üí Security and Account Access ‚Üí Connected Apps
  3. Re-authorize the extension through the settings panel
  4. Ensure third-party cookies are enabled in browser settings
  5. Check if your X account has any restrictions or suspensions
  6. Try using a different browser to isolate the issue

Video Quality Issues

If embedded videos appear in low quality:

  1. Check the quality settings in the extension dashboard
  2. Verify the original video's quality (we can't improve low-quality sources)
  3. Ensure you have a stable high-speed internet connection
  4. Try adjusting your browser's hardware acceleration settings
  5. Update your graphics drivers if videos appear pixelated
